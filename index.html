About us

We are a group of young professionals and students who are passionate about making a positive difference in the Prince George community and the world.

We meet twice a month, either in-person or virtually, to discuss and participate in various service projects, professional development activities, and social events that align with our values and goals.

<div>What is Rotaract</div>

What is Rotaract?

Rotaract is a global organization supported by Rotary International. It empowers people aged 18 and older to create positive change in their local communities and around the world.

The Prince George Rotaract Club is also sponsored by the three Rotary Clubs in Prince George: Downtown, Nechako, and Yellowhead. They offer us guidance, support, and partnership.

<div>Our vision</div>

Why join Rotaract?

Make an impact

Be a part of a local movement that strives to create positive change in the Prince George community and the world.

Expand your network

Connect with like-minded people who share your passion for service above self by joining a be part of a worldwide network of young leaders.

Build skills

Improve your leadership, communication, teamwork, project management, and more by taking on roles and responsibilities in the club

Have fun

Enjoy social events and activities with your fellow club members, such as dining out, game night, or even travel opportunities.

Personal growth

Step out of your comfort zone by trying new things and learning new skills. Rotaract helps you grow as a person and a leader.

Rotary mentorship

Learn from the experience and guidance of our sponsor Rotary clubs, who are established professionals and community leaders.

Current Projects

People of PG

We spread awareness and bring a face to impacted individuals to reduce the local stigma associated with homelessness. To do so, we invite various community groups to share with us the impact they have on the community and the homeless population.

Charms for Change

We fundraise through the sale of wellness-inspired bundles with locally donated products to benefit the Prince George and District Elizabeth Fry Society. In addition, we hope the partnership can help increase community engagement by partnering with local businesses
