Hoop-A-Thon 2024

In collaboration with the UNBC Timberwolves, join us for the 2024 Hoop-A-Thon! This year's event will be held at the Jumpstart Multi Sport Court on Saturday, June 8th, starting at 10 AM. Registration is now open for teams and individuals!

Add to Google calendar Register now

This is how it works...

Registered individuals or teams can collect pledges from friends, families, or community members before or during the event.

On the event day, each participant has 1 minute to shoot as many free throws as possible. The total number of successful free throws determines the amount raised.

After, donations are tallied and collected, either in cash or online, and the funds will be used to support youth at risk, families living in poverty, and Rotary community projects in Prince George.

About the organizers, the Rotary Clubs

The five Rotary Clubs in Prince George collaborate, and encourage one another, in sponsoring the creation of recreational spaces, infrastructure, and public services. You may recognize community-based projects such as the Rotary Soccer Fields, the Rotary Skate Park, the Rotary Baseball Diamonds, the Rotary Community Garden, and many more.

Supporting Hoop-A-Thon is supporting the great work of Rotary within our community! All funds gathered will go towards programs centered on youth, families at risk, and ongoing Rotary programs.
