Shining a Spotlight on Humanity: The People of PG Project

With compassion as our compass, we initiated the "People of PG" project to spread awareness regarding the reality of available social services and to bring a face to impacted individuals in the hope of reducing the local stigma associated with homelessness. Throughout the year, four inspiring speakers graced our meetings, sharing their stories and igniting a collective passion for service and empathy. We continue to welcome speakers to share their perspectives on homelessness in Prince George and share idea on how the Rotaract Club can make a positive impact.
